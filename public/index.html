About Me

Hey there. My name is Nancy, and I'm a current Senior at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. Don't know where that is? No worries, I didn't either before starting college there. If you picture Pennsylvania like a long rectangle, with Philadelphia at the bottom right corner, then Pittsburgh is almost at the bottom left corner -- pretty far away! Fun fact: with its many rivers and hills and ravines, Pittsburgh is known as "The City of Bridges", and is the number 2 city in the US with the most number of bridges apart from NYC!

At CMU, I am a Computer Science major with a minor in Business. Another fun fact: I actually started out the other way around -- as a business major intending to minor/double major in CS, and switched at the end of my Sophomore year. Before college, I had spent the first 14 years of my life growing up in Beijing, China, and moved to Seattle, WA for high school. All my family still live in China, and I go back to visit them every break.

In my free time, I love traveling and photography. I studided B&W photography in high school for 3 years, and have been the unofficial photographer for my family on trips for years. :P You can check out some of the pictures I've taken here. I also love watching TV -- Modern Family is one of my all-time favorites. I’m currently following Suits, and anxiously awaiting the return of Silicon Valley, Game of Thrones, and Orange Is The New Black.
